Privacy Policy for Expense App

Last updated: 3/20/2024

At Expense App, accessible from https://expenseapp.devopschad.com/, one of our main priorities is the privacy of our visitors. This Privacy Policy document contains types of information that are not collected and recorded by Expense App and how we use it.

If you have additional questions or require more information about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us chjthomps@gmail.com.

General Information

Expense App provides an online expense reporting tool that allows users to generate Excel files based on their input. Our application is designed to process input temporarily to generate these files and does not store any personal data, input, or generated files.

Information We Do Not Collect

Expense App does not collect any personal data from its users. Users are invited to input data to generate Excel files, but this data is processed on-the-fly and is not stored, recorded, collected, or shared with any third parties.

Use of Your Information

Since Expense App does not collect or store any data, there are no uses of such data to disclose in this Privacy Policy.

Security of Your Information

To ensure the highest level of security, Expense App operates entirely without storing user data. By not retaining data beyond the processing required to generate Excel files, we inherently minimize any risk associated with data storage and protect our users' privacy.

Online Privacy Policy Only

This Privacy Policy applies only to our online activities and is valid for visitors to our application with regards to the information that they shared and/or input in Expense App. This policy does not apply to any information collected offline or via channels other than this application.
